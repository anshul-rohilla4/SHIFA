About SHIFA

Our Goal

The project's goal is to make Sadholi Block resilient and empowered to exercise their mental health and disability rights fully.

Our Framework

Enhancing the potential of individuals with psychosocial disabilities and their families to achieve fulfilling and meaningful lives through empowerment and support.

Our Pathways

Bridging gaps, empowering care, supporting mental health, using visuals, fostering leadership.

Read More
  • Our initiatives focus on task-shifting with lay health workers, empowering individuals and families as care experts, providing home-based psychosocial support, using pictorial aids to address mental health determinants, and developing community leadership for project ownership.

SHIFA Mental Health and Disability Project

As part of SHIFA-II, the Community mental Health & Disability project was launched in year 2012 in 35 Gram Panchayats of Sadholi Kadim Development Block, Saharanpur District, Uttar Pradesh.

The project is supported by TEAR Australia. The growing issue of broken relationship, difficulties to cope with fast raging development and social changes, increase dependency on drugs and alcohol, poverty, suicide and increase burden of psycho-somatic disorder pushed CHD to initiate long term Mental Health interventions.
